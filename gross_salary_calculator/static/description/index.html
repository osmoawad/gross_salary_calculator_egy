Gross Salary Calculator for Egypt (Odoo 13-18)

This module allows HR and payroll departments to accurately calculate gross salary from net salary based on the Egyptian tax law of 2025.

💡 Features

  • Net to Gross salary calculation based on official tax brackets.
  • Includes Martyrs Fund (0.05%) and Social Insurance (11%).
  • Supports income tax with precise rounding down to 10 piastres.
  • Checkboxes to exclude Insurance, Tax, or Martyrs Fund.
  • Works directly inside the HR Contract form.
  • Compatible with Odoo 13.0 to 18.0 (Community & Enterprise).

🧾 Tax Formula Logic (2025)

  • Social Insurance: 11% capped at 14,500 EGP
  • Martyrs Fund: 0.05% of gross salary
  • Income Tax: progressive brackets with 20,000 EGP exemption

📦 How It Works

  1. Go to Employees → Contracts.
  2. Create or open a contract.
  3. Enter Net Salary.
  4. The system calculates the Gross Salary automatically.

✔️ Why You Need It

  • Ensure the employee receives the exact agreed amount.
  • Maintain tax & insurance compliance.
  • Simplify HR payroll configuration.
