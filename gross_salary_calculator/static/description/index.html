Gross Salary Calculator for Odoo

This module calculates gross salary from net salary based on the Egyptian payroll law (2025).

Features:

  • Supports social insurance ceiling (14,500 EGP)
  • Calculates income tax based on 2024 slabs
  • Includes Martyrs' Families Fund (0.05%)
  • Works from Odoo 13 to 18

Just enter the net salary, and the module will reverse calculate the gross salary, ready to use in contracts and payslips.
